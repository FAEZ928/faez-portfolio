Simple Blog Layout

The Simple Blog Layout project highlights my ability to create a clean, user-friendly blog that emphasizes readability and easy navigation. The main goal was to design a platform for sharing thoughts and insights on various topics.

The blog displays posts on the homepage with a featured image, title, and excerpt, allowing visitors to quickly browse content. HTML and CSS ensure an organized layout with clear headings and an intuitive design.

Each post links to its own page, where readers can fully engage with the content, enhanced by thoughtful typography and visuals. A comments section invites reader interaction, and a sidebar offers links to popular posts, categories, and archives for easy exploration.

Responsive design principles were applied to maintain usability on all devices, from desktops to phones. This project demonstrates my skills in HTML, CSS, and PHP, showcasing my commitment to building engaging, functional websites.

Key Features:

  • Well-structured layout displaying multiple posts on the homepage.
  • Individual post pages with detailed articles and visuals.
  • Comments section for reader engagement and interaction.
  • Sidebar with links to popular posts, categories, and archives.
  • Responsive design for readability on all devices.
